About Studio Co.llab

Studio Co.llab is a digital-first, independent strategy and design company. Our small, but mighty strategist and designer team work collaboratively with clients to help solve problems and craft actionable solutions at every stage — from strategic thinking to design to execution.
Our creative approach is simple - we take the time to listen and learn about what you have to offer, craft exciting and authentic ideas true to your mission and deliver unique digital experiences to make your brand stand out from the rest - all without the overhead of an agency.

Whether you’re starting from scratch or pivoting direction, we love helping companies imagine all their possibilities. We’ll partner with you, step-by-step to turn your vision into reality - even if that vision isn’t fully shaped yet. Let’s get to work!

Meet the Founders

About Team Nicol(l)e

Nicole and Nicolle met back in 2012 as young, fresh minds in the advertising world. After what felt like thousands of late night pitches and big name digital campaigns, each decided it was time to pursue something different.

Nicolle dove deep into the strategy and consulting space, working with exciting and notable Fortune 500 clients. Nicole spent time exploring the in’s and out’s of in-house product design, creative leadership and eventually transitioning to a full time freelancer working with a variety of clients, big and small.

As time went on, the two found themselves with an opportunity to work on a project together again. Both realized, with all the skills and knowledge they had collected over the years they could embark on a new journey as two woman team, working with clients who genuinely care about creating great digital experiences with them.
