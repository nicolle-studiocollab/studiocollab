Designing a Digital Loyalty Program to Drive Engagement and Growth

theSkimm has transformed the way millions of women read the news & make decisions. Studio Co.llab helped relaunch the Skimm's loyalty program that would deepen the brand relationship and fuel referrals & growth.

Project Objectives

theSkimm was in the early stages of evolving their Skimm'bassador program from a "referral" program to a loyalty experience that would allow members to earn and redeem points while interacting with theSkimm's unique content.

theSkimm engaged Studio Co.llab to craft a loyalty program positioning & strategy that resonated with their target audiences and served as a "brief" for Studio Co.llab to design the digital loyalty program experience.

A test and learn approach infused user insights throughout the strategy & user experience to ensure the loyalty experience delivers on audience needs & remains authentic to theSkimm's unique brand.

Services

Trends to Inspire Program Positioning

Before we could design the experience, we needed to answer "why would users want to sign up for theSkimm loyalty program?"

We combined hypotheses based on the existing Skimm'bassador program with a wide exploration of digital loyalty, gamification, and membership experiences to lead a cross-functional 'brainstorm' with theSkimm team members.

Outputs from the brainstorm lead to three unique positioning concepts that we refined into stimulus to test with theSkimm users.

Visual Moodboarding & Dashboard Design

Using insights from positioning concept testing, we compiled a variety of visual inspiration into ‘moodboards’ to align the team on a visual direction.

Once a visual direction was chosen, we applied the visual system to a 'key screen', the loyalty Dashboard, to set the stage for all UX & UI design of all other screens.

Audience "Tiers" & Loyalty Strategy

theSkimm wanted to define loyalty "tiers" to incentivize engagement. By defining a unique 'persona' for each tier, we made sure the program resonnated with the full breadth of loyalty members.

Findings were documented in a cohesive "Loyalty Strategy" that defined audiences, program goals, and "MVP vs. Future State" experiences to support internal socialization & onboarding.

User-Vetted Responsive Design with UX & UI

We began designing the full loyalty experience with UX wireframes to drive alignment and select functionality. These high fidelity wireframes were tested with users and gather experience feedback.

A component-based visual system helped break down design elements for easier engineering and future experience maintenance.

The visual UI was applied to the final mobile and desktop screens and aligned to the direction from the visual exploration & existing theSkimm brand. We worked with the engineering team to define final requirements & assets for hand-off.
