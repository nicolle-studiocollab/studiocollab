Driving Brand Awareness with a Social Strategy Playbook

As a B2B company offering Experience Management solutions, Qualtrics was looking for to build strategic and creative frameworks that would immediately jumpstart new social posts that would interest and drive awareness with their end-users.

Project Objectives

Qualtrics wanted to reinvigorate its Social presence to pivot away from traditional B2B-Selling and speak more to end-users and future talent while positioning its brand as cultivating "breakthrough experiences and those bold enough to chase them."

Qualtrics engaged Studio Co.llab to create a social strategy 'playbook' that could define a common social presence, with content "pillars" and sample social posts that leverage social trends, audience interests, and Qualtrics' unique offers to increase brand awareness and love.

Services

Social Media Strategy, Concepting, Market Reserach, User Research, Creative Direction, Visual Design

Timeline

6 Weeks

Industry

Experience Management

Sprint 1

Strategy and Trends to Spark Ideas

  • Internal interviews to surface hypotheses for audience needs & brand messages we can highlight.
  • Social trends found across social platforms from brands in and out of the experience management category.
  • "Ideation Brief" to define Social goals, channel focus, target audience, and idea starters.

Sprint 2

Ideate, Refine, and Test Social 'Concepts'

  • Virtual brainstorm with 20+ cross-functional teammembers to generate ideas & combine into themes.
  • Refined 3 concepts with concept manifestos and social post ideas to test with audiences.
  • Interviews with Studio Co.llab's peer network of Marketing Professionals to gather reactions & learnings.

Sprint 3

Visual Direction and Final Strategy

  • "Social Strategy Framework" with User Needs, Final Direction, and Social Activation 'Themes'
  • Creative Direction Applied Across Social Channels
  • Representative Posts Using Visual Direction & Existing Qualtrics Content to Show How to Apply Activation 'Themes'
