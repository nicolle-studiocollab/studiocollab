What
We Offer

Our creative approach is simple - we take the time to listen and learn about what you have to offer, craft exciting and authentic ideas true to your mission and deliver unique digital experiences to make your brand stand out - all without the overhead of an agency.

01.

Discovery + Exploration

Forming a brief, plus some. We merge outside-exploration, user research, and design thinking methods to get to come up with early concepts while creating a strategic foundation.

02.

Strategy + Ideation

All about distilling ideas to define a focus. We hypothesize concepts and bring them to life just enough to vet them with users & internal stakeholders. Call this ‘iterative’ call it ‘agile’ - basically we keep going until it’s right.

03.

Design + Creative Direction

Where the work gets real - we’ll design out the UX & UI across different user flows and bring your concepts to life. As we do this, we codify guidelines & design systems to help future design work.

04.

Management + Support

We know what it takes to get designs executed. We stay-on as an advisory partner to ensure the strategy & design direction gets pulled through the ups and downs of build & launch.

How We're Different

Intentionally Small

As a team of two, you’ll work with us directly from strategic thinking all the way to creative execution. We give our clients our dedicated focus and make their priorities our priorities.

Extension of Your Team

We embed ourselves directly into your day-to-day. Whether it’s attending daily stand-ups, meetings or dropping the casual dad joke in Slack, we’ll do whatever it takes to make working as seamless and fun as possible.

Female Founded & Led

We’re a female team, making us a rarity in the digital experience world. We bring a different perspective and approach to digital thinking, leadership and execution.

Actionable Next Steps

We make taking the next step easy. Whether it’s roadmapping a plan with development teams, or creating detailed playbooks, our work leaves you ready to launch with confidence.

About Team Nicol(l)e

Nicole and Nicolle met back in 2012 as young, fresh minds in the advertising world. After what felt like thousands of late night pitches and big name digital campaigns, each decided it was time to pursue something different.

Nicolle dove deep into the strategy and consulting space, working with exciting and notable Fortune 500 clients. Nicole spent time exploring the in’s and out’s of in-house product design, creative leadership and eventually transitioning to a full time freelancer working with a variety of clients, big and small.

As time went on, the two found themselves with an opportunity to work on a project together again. Both realized, with all the skills and knowledge they had collected over the years they could embark on a new journey as two woman team, working with clients who genuinely care about creating great digital experiences with them.
